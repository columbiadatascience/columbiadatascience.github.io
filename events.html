Testing at Etsy, with Hilary Parker

Date/Time: Tuesday, February 3rd @ 7:45pm
Location: TBA

Etsy Senior Data Analyst Hilary Parker will give an introduction to testing at Etsy. Etsy is well known for investing heavily in A/B testing their website, and this talk will give you both an inside view into how they work, and the tools to get started for your own projects. Just in time for ADI's DevFest!

Hilary is a Senior Data Analyst at Etsy, where she helps product teams with data-informed development via experimentation, opportunity sizing and impact analysis.

She recently got her Ph.D. in Biostatistics at the Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health, working with Jeff Leek. There she studied genomics, and built tools to help researchers use genomic technologies in personalized medicine applications. Prior she graduated from Pomona College in 2008, where she double-majored in Mathematics and Molecular Biology.
